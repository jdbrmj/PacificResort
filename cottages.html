The Cottages at Pacific trails

what is cottage?

Our luxury cottages are permanent stuctures four feet off the ground. Each cottage has canvas walls, a wooden
floor, a roof dome that can be opened, and private deck

How are the cottage furnished?

Each cottage is furnished with gas fireplace, king-size bed with down quit, comfy couch, dining table and
chairs, minu-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker. Your luxury camping experience includes a sink with
hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should i bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to drees for changing weather with light layers of
clothing. it's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure.
